National Day

Qatar National Day is a celebration to commemorate the founding of the State of Qatar by the founder Sheikh Jassem Bin Mohammed on 18 December 1878 and celebrated on December 18 of each year. It was decided to be considered a holiday by a decree issued by Prince and Crown Prince Sheikh Tamim bin Hamad Al Thani on 21 June 2007.

Qatar's National Day affirms the identity and history of the state. It embodies the ideals and hopes of the state, and also honors the men and women who participated in the building of the state, in particular the Al-Thani family.

Qatar's National Day is an opportunity to learn about the work of the founders of the State of Qatar, who have endured difficulties and paid dearly for the unity and commemoration of their nation.

The celebration aims at introducing Qatar's heritage and appreciation and promoting pride in Qatar.
